* **Routine Care Challenges:** The intense commitment required for managing medical follow-ups, medication, and overall physical care.
* **Anxieties About Safety:** The constant worry regarding the child’s safety and survival during disasters (e.g., floods, fires) if a caregiver is not around.
* **Financial Burden:** High costs associated with cardiac care, surgeries, and continuous pediatric check-ups.
* **Community and Government Aid:** The vital role of local barangay programs, NDPR Week, and financial support in helping parents cope.
* **Personal Acceptance:** The emotional struggle of the parents upon learning of the child's conditions at birth and their journey toward acceptance.
